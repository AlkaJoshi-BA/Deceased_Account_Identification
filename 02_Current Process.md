Current Process in Commercial Banks - In most commercial banks, the bank does not usually receive automatic information when a customer passes away. Therefore, the account continues to remain active until the bank is officially informed and the necessary documents are verified. The family member or nominee informs the bank. Bank verifies documents such as the death certificate. After verification customer status is updated in the Customer Information File (CIF). The bank applies transaction restrictions on the account. Nominee or legal heir settlement process begins.
